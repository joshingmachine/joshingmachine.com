About

Hi! I'm Josh Smith. I'm passionate about making opportunities accessible to everyone, and I believe a crucial part of that is education.

In service of that, I work as an engineering manager for the front end engineering team at Niche, a website and app that helps people find the colleges, schools, and places to live that are right for them.

I live in Pittsburgh with my wife and our muppet of a dog. Some of my interests include:

  • reading (books, articles, essays, humor, history, engineering and business practices, soft sci-fi and fantasy)
  • Pixar movies
  • cooking (especially savory breakfast foods)
  • watching stand-up comedy

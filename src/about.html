About

Hi! I'm Josh Smith.

I work as an engineering manager for the Front End Engineering team at Niche, a platform that helps people find the colleges, schools, and places to live that are right for them.

I'm a lifelong resident of Pittsburgh, PA, where I live with my wife and our muppet of a dog.

Some of my interests include:

  • spending quality time with family and friends
  • reading (most genres and formats)
  • comedy (consuming standup and satire)
  • games (mostly the board and Nintendo varieties)
  • code, startups, and tech (especially tech ethics and EdTech)
  • playing guitar
  • Pixar movies (I have strong feelings about Toy Story 4)

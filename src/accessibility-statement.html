Accessibility Statement

I want this website and the content on it to be accessible to as many people as possible.

In that effort, I aim to comply with all applicable standards. At the minimum, I strive to conform with Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA.

This webpage is a living document. On it I will describe what efforts I take to ensure accessibility, as well as acknowledge any known issues with this website.

If you encounter an issue on this website that makes it or the content on it harder to access, please file a GitHub Issue on the repo or contact me. I take these issues very seriously, and will address any known issues as promptly as possible.
